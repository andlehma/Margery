DECISION MAKING
===============
if ball is above crossbar
	wait
else
	if ball is in offensive half
		if self in position to shoot
			shoot
		else
			get in position
	else
		go_to_location(car, goal) # far post?
		if ball is inside goal box
			shoot
		else
			wait


METHODS
=======
go_to_location(car, target)
	aim car towards target (implement half-flip)
	drive until distance to target is under some threshold
	aim car at ball

shoot(car, ball)
	go_to_location(car, ball) # higher threshold than normal
	jump and dodge forward quickly

get_in_position(car, ball)
	get line from center of goal to ball
	go_to_location(car, nearest point on that line)
